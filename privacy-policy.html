Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026  ·  Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

AI Tools for Pros ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at aitoolsforpros.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data. We have written this policy in plain language because we think you deserve to understand it.

The short version: we collect minimal data, we use standard analytics to understand how people use the site, we do not sell your information to anyone, and we do not use your data for advertising targeting.

Information we collect

Information you give us directly: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. If you contact us, we collect whatever information you include in that message. We do not collect your name, phone number, address, or any other personal information unless you voluntarily provide it.

Information collected automatically: Like most websites, we use analytics software to collect standard usage data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query that brought you here
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • General geographic location (country and region level, not precise location)
  • IP address (anonymized before storage)

This data is aggregated and used only to understand site usage trends. It is not linked to individual user profiles.

How we use your information

  • Email address: To send the newsletter you subscribed to, and occasional product announcements. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email.
  • Analytics data: To understand which content is useful, identify technical problems, and improve the site. We do not use this data for advertising.
  • Contact messages: To respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list based on contact form submissions.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for analytics and to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics. Google may process data on servers outside your country. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Email newsletter provider: We use a third-party email platform to manage newsletter subscriptions and delivery.

We do not share your data with any other third parties for marketing, advertising, or data-brokering purposes.

Affiliate links

Some links on this website are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission if you purchase through them. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial assessments. When affiliate links are present, they are disclosed on the relevant page.

Data retention

Email subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely in aggregated form. Contact form submissions are deleted after we have responded to your inquiry.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (including unsubscribing from our newsletter)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We will notify newsletter subscribers of material changes by email.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the About Us page. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.

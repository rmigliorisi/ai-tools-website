Common Questions

Everything you need to know about how AI Tools for Pros works.

What is AI Tools for Pros?

AI Tools for Pros is an independent directory and review site that helps working professionals find the right AI tools for their specific jobs. Unlike general tech review sites, we evaluate tools through the lens of each profession, considering factors like compliance requirements, workflow fit, accuracy, and real-world performance. We cover tools like Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and hundreds of specialized AI applications across industries including law, medicine, real estate, finance, engineering, and more.

How do you test and review AI tools?

Every tool we review goes through hands-on testing using real professional tasks, not benchmarks designed to make tools look good. For legal tools, we run actual contract review and research tasks. For medical tools, we evaluate clinical documentation and diagnostic support in realistic workflows. We document what the tool does well, where it fails, and what professionals need to know before trusting it with important work.

Are your reviews paid for or sponsored?

No. We do not accept payment for reviews, rankings, or featured placements. AI tool companies cannot buy their way into a positive review or onto our recommended list. Our editorial independence is the whole point. Professionals need honest information, not marketing copy dressed up as a review. If that ever changes, we'll be transparent about it.

Which AI tools are best for professionals in 2026?

It depends entirely on your profession and the specific tasks you want help with. For legal professionals, tools like Harvey AI and Clio Duo are leading the field. For general professional productivity, Claude and ChatGPT remain the most capable general-purpose AI assistants. For research and real-time information, Perplexity AI is widely used.
